Multiple activators for the same element

Hover on me, or me, or me to highlight a box!
Multiple activators can highlight the same element by having a matching data-lazy-highlighter-activator attribute.

Alternatively, highlighted element can have a joint
data-lazy-highlighter-id property, consisting of multiple space-separated activator properties. 
<div style="width:50%">
    Hover on 
    <span data-lazy-highlighter-activator="demo_1-many">me</span>
    to highlight multiple boxes!
</div>
<div style="width:50%">
    <div data-lazy-highlighter-id="demo_1-many">Box 1 has a pink border.</div>
    <div data-lazy-highlighter-id="demo_1-many">Box 2 has a blue border.</div>
    <div data-lazy-highlighter-id="demo_1-many">Box 3 has a yellow border.</div>
</div>
