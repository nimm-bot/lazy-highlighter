Multiple activators and highlighted elements

Hover on me, and me, and me to highlight a corresponding box!
Elements can be highlighted anywhere in the same document, as long as highlighted element's data-lazy-highlighter-id matches activator's data-lazy-highlighter-activator.
In case of iframes, lazy highlighter must be initialized for each iframe's inner document, but elements outside said iframe cannot be accessed.
